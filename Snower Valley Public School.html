Introduction

Established in 2003, Snower Valley Senior Secondary School works on the philosophy that education is not preparation for life, it is life itself. In the area of academics, sports and co-curricular activities it has always been a recognizable force at state and national level. The untiring endeavors of Management have enabled the school to scale new glories and achieve distinction in the state.

The school is equipped with facilities of all sorts that are necessary to impart quality education to the students. It has Science Laboratories (Physics, Chemistry & Biology), Atal Tinkering Lab, Smart Classrooms, Advanced Library, Multimedia Computer Laboratory, Wi-Fi Campus, CCTV surveillance, OMR Setup, Educational Software, School Management Software, spacious classrooms, a big playground, transport facility, hygienic sanitation, RO water facility and many more.

The utmost dedication and hard work of the Management, Principal, teachers and students have brought laurels to the school in every sphere. Great encouragement is given to students in academics, sports and co-curricular activities. The school organizes Quiz Competitions, Debate & Speech Competitions, Drawing & Painting Competitions, Science Exhibitions, Sports Meets and many other events. The school has proudly achieved 51 Merits in 22 concluding sessions. The school campus is located at a centralized position and is well connected by roads, making transportation easy for students. We assure every student the best education and help them build a bright future.
