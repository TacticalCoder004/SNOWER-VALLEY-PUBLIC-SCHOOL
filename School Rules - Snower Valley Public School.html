School Rules & Regulations

🚌 Discipline in the Bus

  • Stand in the queue to get into the bus.
  • Do not push other students. Sit in the seat allotted to you.
  • Do not walk around in the moving bus.
  • Do not break segregation rules in the bus.
  • Do not shout or talk loudly as it may distract the driver.
  • Do not fight with other students.
  • Do not throw litter inside or outside the bus.
  • Keep your bags properly and do not block the aisle.
  • Do not damage the bus interiors.
  • Respect the bus staff and fellow students.
  • Be at the bus stop at least 5 minutes before the scheduled time.
  • Follow all transport rules issued by the school.

📚 School Library Rules

  • Maintain complete silence inside the library.
  • Only one book will be issued per library card.
  • Books are not issued during vacations.
  • Return books within one week.
  • Late return of books will attract a fine.
  • Lost books must be replaced by the borrower.
  • Do not write, underline or tear pages of library books.
  • Handle books carefully and return them in good condition.
